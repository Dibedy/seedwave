What is a Seedwave?

A Seedwave is an interesting phenomenon in Minecraft Java Edition. It represents the current distribution of good seeds generated by the Random Seed Generator (RSG).

The strength of a Seedwave indicates how likely it is to encounter favorable worlds for speedrunning, with higher strengths signaling a greater presence of optimal seeds.

The strength of a Seedwave can vary between 1 and 10.
